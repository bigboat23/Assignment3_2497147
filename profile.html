About Me

As a football enthusiast, I am a huge fan of Manchester United, and I admire players like Marcus Rashford for their incredible skills and impact on and off the field.

Another passion of mine is high-performance sports cars and hypercars. I'm particularly fascinated by the Pagani Huayra, which I consider a true work of art. The combination of engineering excellence and exquisite design truly captivates me.

In addition to that, I have a keen interest in technology. I believe in the power of technology to improve lives and create a more connected world. I envision a future where cutting-edge technology is accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial resources, and where innovation is driven by a unified vision rather than just profit margins.

Music is another creative outlet for me. I enjoy both listening to music and creating my own. It allows me to express myself and tap into emotions in a unique way.
