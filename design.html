UI Description

Home Page
The home page features a hero section that immediately captures the user's attention with compelling text and a short description of what they can expect on other parts of the site.
This design choice aligns with Papanek's notion that design should be revolutionary and socially responsible. By presenting a clear overview of the site's content, we aim to engage users and provide them with a meaningful experience.


Portfolio Page
The portfolio section showcases a collection of images arranged in a horizontal slideshow, resembling a gallery. This design choice draws from Gibson's idea that interfaces should be designed to fit the user's perception and action capabilities.
By presenting the images in a familiar format, users can intuitively click and slide through the portfolio, providing an interactive and engaging experience.


Essay Page
The essay section focuses on the content itself, with the essay text pushed to the middle of the page and divided into paragraphs and sections.
This design choice reflects the importance of functional design highlighted by Papanek. The essay is presented in a clear and organized manner, allowing users to easily read and navigate through the content. The use of a vertical scroll aligns with conventional web design practices, providing a familiar browsing experience for users.


Design Page
The design page follows a vertical scroll layout, presenting different sections detailing various design aspects.
While adhering to common conventions, we have aimed to make these sections the best they can be in terms of functionality and usability. This approach resonates with Papanek's idea of design being socially responsible by prioritizing functionality over form. By presenting the information in a clear and easily digestible manner, we ensure that users can access the content effortlessly.

Profile Page

The profile page maintains consistency with the common conventions of profile pages, ensuring users are familiar with the layout and interaction patterns.
While the design may not be considered radical or ground-breaking, our focus has been on functionality and user experience. By incorporating familiar design elements, we aim to create a seamless and intuitive browsing experience for users.
